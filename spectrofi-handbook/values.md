# Transparency

We make a system that enables to kill more people in a defence use case. The technology itself is neither good nor bad; rather, it is the humans using the tech who choose how it is used. It is our responsibility to ensure that products are not sold to hostile actors who oppose Western values. 

There is trend of making [Open Startups](https://open-startup.org), yeah we are not open startup. Our commitment to transparency does not mean that our codebase, machine learning models, internal policies, and metrics are publicly available, for anybody.
